# Project Vision — Student Success Platform (SSP)

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-15
**Status:** Foundation Phase

---

## Mission Statement

Build a comprehensive academic and career success platform that accompanies students
from their first day at university until graduation and career readiness.

---

## Target Users

| User Type        | Description                                                    |
|------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| Student          | Primary user — undergraduate/graduate university student       |
| Academic Advisor | Faculty member who monitors and guides student progress        |
| Career Counselor | Professional who assists students in career planning           |
| University Admin | Institutional administrator with oversight and reporting needs |
| Mentor           | Industry professional volunteering to mentor students          |
| Employer         | Company posting opportunities and searching for talent         |

---

## Core Problems Being Solved

1. **Fragmented student journey** — Students use disconnected tools for scheduling, grades, career prep
2. **Reactive advising** — Advisors only intervene after problems arise, not proactively
3. **Career unreadiness** — Students graduate without professional identity or job-search skills
4. **Lost opportunities** — Students miss scholarships, internships, and events due to lack of discovery
5. **Institutional blindspot** — Universities lack real-time data on student success indicators
6. **Skill gaps** — No structured path for students to identify and close competency gaps

---

## Strategic Goals

### Short-Term (6 months)
- [ ] Complete platform foundation and architecture
- [ ] Launch Academic module (study plans, GPA tracking, course management)
- [ ] Launch Productivity module (tasks, goals, schedule)
- [ ] Launch Shared module (auth, users, notifications)

### Mid-Term (12 months)
- [ ] Launch Skills and CareerProfile modules
- [ ] Launch Opportunities discovery module
- [ ] Launch Guidance module with AI-powered recommendations
- [ ] Onboard first pilot university

### Long-Term (24 months)
- [ ] Launch Community and Analytics modules
- [ ] Mobile application (React Native or Flutter)
- [ ] API ecosystem for institutional integrations (LMS, ERP)
- [ ] Multi-university, multi-tenant support

## Success Metrics

| Metric                         | Target                        |
|--------------------------------|-------------------------------|
| Student engagement rate        | > 70% weekly active users     |
| Graduation readiness score     | 85% of students on track      |
| Opportunity discovery          | ≥ 5 opportunities per student |
| Advisor response time          | < 24 hours                    |
| Career placement rate          | > 60% within 6 months         |
| Platform uptime                | 99.9% SLA                     |

---

## Non-Goals (explicitly out of scope)

- We do NOT replace Learning Management Systems (LMS) like Moodle or Canvas
- We do NOT handle tuition payments or financial transactions
- We do NOT provide accreditation or certification
- We are NOT a social media platform (community is supplementary, not core)

---

## Guiding Principles

1. **Student-first design** — Every decision serves the student's success
2. **Privacy by default** — Student data is protected; universities never misuse it
3. **Incremental value** — Every feature delivers measurable value immediately
4. **Simplicity over complexity** — No feature for its own sake
5. **Data-driven decisions** — Measure everything, improve continuously
